Anita Desai

Full Name: Anita Mazumdar Desai

Born: June 24, 1937, Mussoorie, India

Nationality: Indian

Profession: Novelist, Short Story Writer, Academic

Language: English

Notable Works: *Clear Light of Day*, *In Custody*, *Fasting, Feasting*

Awards: Sahitya Akademi Award, Guardian Prize

Nominations: Shortlisted three times for the Booker Prize

Known For: Psychological insight and sensitive portrayal of Indian middle-class life
