Mulk Raj Anand

Full Name: Mulk Raj Anand

Born: December 12, 1905, Peshawar, British India (now in Pakistan)

Died: September 28, 2004, Pune, India

Nationality: Indian

Profession: Novelist, Essayist, Art Critic

Language: English

Notable Works: *Untouchable*, *Coolie*, *The Sword and the Sickle*

Themes: Social injustice, caste discrimination, poverty, colonial India

Awards: Sahitya Akademi Award, Padma Bhushan (1967)

Known For: Championing the underprivileged and pioneering Indian-English fiction
