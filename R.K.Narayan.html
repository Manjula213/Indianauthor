R.K. Narayan

Full Name: Rasipuram Krishnaswami Iyer Narayanaswami

Born: October 10, 1906, Madras (now Chennai), India

Died: May 13, 2001, Chennai, India

Profession: Novelist, Short Story Writer

Language: English

Famous Works: *Swami and Friends*, *The Guide*, *Malgudi Days*

Awards: Padma Bhushan (1964), Padma Vibhushan (2000)

Known For: Creating the fictional South Indian town of Malgudi
